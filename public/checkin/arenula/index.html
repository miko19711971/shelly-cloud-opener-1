Arriving at the building

Using the intercom

Press C8 on the intercom panel.

03

Open the building door

Push the door once it is unlocked.

Use this button only when you are physically in front of the main wooden entrance door.
Corridor and stairs04
04

Inside the building

Walk straight across the corridor into the courtyard, then take the stairs on your right after the elevator.

The elevator is strictly private — we are not allowed to use it.
Apartment door — 3rd floor05
05

This is your apartment door on the 3rd floor

On the 3rd floor, attached to the handle, you will find a grey lockbox. The code is 6-7-9-3. Inside you will find the keys: open the cover, take the keys, close the cover again, and scramble the numbers.

Important: after taking the keys, close the lockbox and scramble the numbers.
